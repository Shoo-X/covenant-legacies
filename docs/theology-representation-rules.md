# Covenant: Legacies Theology Representation Rules

Covenant: Legacies represents biblical supernatural themes through covenant, testimony, memory, prayer, judgment, witness, and divine intervention. The tone should remain serious, reverent, ancient, mysterious, and family friendly.

## Core Rules

- The player does not control God, the Holy Spirit, or divine power as a magic system.
- The Holy Spirit should not be a collectible card, unit, resource object, or upgrade currency.
- Angels should generally be represented as divine intervention, message, judgment, or protection, not as pets or player-controlled creatures.
- Biblical witnesses should be treated reverently as testimony, legacy, covenant memory, or witness cards, not as disposable units.
- Forbidden spiritual content should be framed as warning, temptation, tragedy, corruption, or costly deception, not as a valid wisdom path.
- Speculative fiction must remain clearly distinct from Scripture.
- Scripture, Interpretive Tradition, and Speculative Fiction source tiers must remain visible in card, codex, and encounter data.

## Sensitive Representation Examples

- David vs Goliath: legacy and courage, grounded in trust and covenant deliverance rather than spectacle.
- Mary Magdalene at the Empty Tomb: witness and hope, not a magical unit or combat summon.
- Archangel Michael: intervention and judgment under delegated divine authority, not a summoned fantasy creature.
- Melchizedek: mystery and covenant blessing, not a normal controllable unit.
- The Medium at Endor: forbidden warning, tragic and cautionary, not endorsed occult practice.
- Forbidden Watcher Diagram: forbidden warning, dangerous and corrupting rather than glamorous.
- Blessing of the Most High: covenant memory and blessing, with God Most High as the source of authority.

## Design Vocabulary

Prefer language such as:

- Covenant
- Witness
- Legacy
- Prayer
- Judgment
- Divine intervention
- Testimony
- Remembrance
- Blessing
- Warning

Avoid language that makes holy or angelic figures feel like controllable fantasy summons, pets, spell components, or collectible power objects.

## Metadata

Source-backed content may use `representationMode` metadata to clarify intent. Current supported values are:

- `Legacy`
- `Witness`
- `Intervention`
- `Prayer`
- `CovenantMemory`
- `MysteryEncounter`
- `ForbiddenWarning`
- `Enemy`
- `SpeculativeEnemy`
